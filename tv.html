This is a Knight TV console emulator. It's connected to a PDP-11 emulator running the TV-11 software, which in turn talks to a PDP-10 emulator running ITS.

When you connect, you will automatically be assigned a free display/keyboard pair. This assignment will last until you click Disconnect, or close the browser window.

In order to faithfully emulate a Knight keyboard, the application grabs the keyboard. ESC maps to ALTMODE, Alt to META, F1 to CALL, and F2 to ESC. Calling an elevator or buzzing the door will have no effect... yet.
